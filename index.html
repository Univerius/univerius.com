We provide a direct observation experience of the Moon, the Sun, and planets via telescopes in various city squares in Italy and abroad.

With us you see the details of craters on the Moon, protuberances on the Sun, the great red spot on Jupiter, the circles of Saturn, and 50-shades of red of Mars.
